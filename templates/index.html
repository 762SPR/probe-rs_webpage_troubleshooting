probe-rs

a modern, embedded debugging toolkit, written in Rust to preserve your sanity

Flash programming

Downloading a binary to your MCU is easy with probe-rs. We support ELF, ihex or plain binary.

Architecture & probe independent

ARM, RiscV, CMSIS-DAP, STLink, JLink, and many more to to come probe-rs supports it all.

Cargo integration

With cargo-flash you get everything cargo run gives you, but for embedded targets. No compromises.

Extensible

probe-rs can be used as a library, giving you even more flexibility.

GDB integration

probe-rs includes a GDB stub to integrate seamlessly into your usual workflow.

VSCode integration

A modern debugging toolkit requires a modern editor, which is why we provide a plugin for our favorite one.
